What is a Green Space?

A green space is any open space or peice of land that has not been developed i.e. has no buildings or structures on it. They may include:

  1. School Playgrounds

  2. Parks

  3. Recreational Grounds

  4. Public Seated Areas

  5. Community Gardens

Why do we care?

It is important that we promote the use of outdoor green spaces to help tackle important global issues! With the rise of obesity and impending climatic change, we need to encourage and enable people to rediscover our natural world!

Research has found that mental health and general wellbeing is greatly increased when there is an increase in the time spent in nature. Even a 20 minute walk is enough to make a significant difference to our moods! Through the use of The Green Space Place it is possible to track down your nearest greenspace where you can go for a walk or picnic in your spare time. Nature is far closer than you'd first think!
